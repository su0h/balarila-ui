Try Balarila

What is Balarila?

Balarila is a Filipino Grammar Error Correction (GEC) model, developed as a proof of concept through advanced deep learning research. Its core purpose is to demonstrate how deep learning can tackle a specific, complex challenge: correcting semantic errors in Filipino text. Unlike existing rule-based Filipino grammar checkers, Balarila explores the deeper meaning and structural nuances of sentences.

Creating Balarila

Our development process involved overcoming significant hurdles – particularly the scarcity of suitable Filipino text datasets. We created an automated sentence corruption pipeline to create a robust synthetic corpora for our training needs. This enabled us to fine-tune state-of-the-art Tagalog BERT and RoBERTa models significantly better – showcasing the potential for highly accurate Filipino grammar error correction.

Disclaimer

Please be aware that Balarila is a research prototype and is not meant for real-world applications. It serves as a valuable academic contribution – highlighting new possibilities in Natural Language Processing for the Filipino language. For a comprehensive look at Balarila's methodology and evaluation, you can refer to our published research paper.
