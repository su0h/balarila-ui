What is BALARILA?

Balarila is an innovative Filipino Grammar Error Correction (GEC) model, developed as a proof of concept through advanced deep learning research. Its core purpose is to demonstrate how AI can tackle a specific, complex challenge: correcting semantic errors in Filipino text. Unlike common grammar checkers that only fix spelling or punctuation, Balarila explores the deeper meaning and structural nuances of sentences.


Our development process involved overcoming significant hurdles, particularly the scarcity of suitable Filipino grammar datasets. We pioneered an automated error generation pipeline to create robust synthetic corpora. This unique approach enabled the comprehensive training and fine-tuning of state-of-the-art Tagalog BERT and RoBERTa models, showcasing the potential for highly accurate Filipino grammar correction.


Please be aware that Balarila is a research prototype and is not designed for production use or real-world application. It serves as a valuable academic contribution, highlighting new possibilities in Natural Language Processing for the Filipino language and inviting further research and development.


For a comprehensive look at Balarila's methodology and evaluation, please refer to our published research paper: https://aclanthology.org/2023.sealp-1.3.pdf.
